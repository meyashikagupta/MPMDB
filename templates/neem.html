Neem (Azadirachta indica)

Therapeutic properties

  • Potent antibacterial and antifungal activity for skin, scalp, and oral formulations.
  • Anti-inflammatory limonoids that calm eczema, psoriasis, and gingival swelling.
  • Insect-repellent seed oil leveraged in integrated pest-management programmes.

Applications

  • Topical paste or decoction for acne, wound cleansing, and fungal infections.

  • Cold-pressed seed oil for biopesticide sprays and grain storage protection.
  • Leaf extracts blended into nutraceutical detox tonics.
